Journey of Modern Shopping Mall

Located on Mahatma Gandhi Road, Gowripete, Kolar, Modern Shopping Mall has been serving generations since 1950 with trusted fashion collections, traditional wear, wedding collections, and modern branded clothing for the entire family.
