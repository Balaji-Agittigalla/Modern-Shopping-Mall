About Modern Shopping Mall

Elevating Fashion For Every Generation.

Modern Shopping Mall is one of Kolar’s most trusted multi-generational fashion destinations, offering premium collections for men, women, and kids. Located on M.G. BRAND LEGACY

A Fashion Legacy Trusted Across Generations.

Modern Shopping Mall has been a trusted fashion destination in Kolar for decades, offering premium collections for men, women, and kids under one roof. Located on the vibrant M.G. Road in Gowripete, the store has built a strong reputation for quality, affordability, and exceptional customer experience.

From elegant silk sarees and wedding collections to stylish casual wear, designer kidswear, and premium branded apparel, the store continues to evolve with modern fashion trends while preserving the warmth and trust of a family-owned business.

With a carefully curated collection featuring renowned brands like Louis Philippe, U.S. POLO ASSN., Peter England, and more, Modern Shopping Mall has become a preferred shopping destination for families seeking both tradition and contemporary fashion.
